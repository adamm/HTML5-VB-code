Canvas Text

Fonts and Positioning

Synopsis
context.font = 'font-style font-variant font-weight font-size font-family';
context.textAlign = 'text-alignment';
context.textBaseline = 'baseline-alignment';
context.fillText(text, x, y);
context.strokeText(text, x, y);

Note that font-style, font-variant, and font-weight are optional when assigning context.font.
